# 25 — Appendix — Open Questions and Decisions Needed

A single list of everything that needs a CNZ decision before or during the build. Agent, surface these to the product owner — do not guess answers.

## 32.1 Brand and design

- Confirm primary typography (Source Serif 4 + Inter recommended).
- Confirm hex values for all brand colours against brand book.
- Confirm dark mode is not required for v1.
- Confirm hero copy option (A/B/C in section 23.3).
- Confirm new koru colour commissions (coral, plum, gold, navy-reversed).
- Confirm whether any category needs NEW illustration commissions beyond koru variants.

## 32.2 Content and IA

- Resolve sustainable-careers duplicate (under `/funding-and-support/` vs `/development-and-resources/`).
- Confirm promoting Funding calendar into primary nav.
- Confirm new Ngā Toi Māori and Pacific Arts landing pages.
- Confirm the news URL format change (current long timestamp vs new shorter format).
- Confirm which pages are P0/P1 for launch.
- Confirm whether hero A/B testing is desired post-launch.

## 32.3 Technical

- Confirm CMS choice (Sanity vs Payload-on-Azure-NZ).
- Confirm data residency requirements.
- Confirm hosting (Vercel vs Azure Static Web Apps).
- Confirm analytics tool (Plausible vs Fathom vs other).
- Confirm error monitoring (Sentry terms).
- Confirm transactional email (Resend vs existing Exchange relay).
- Confirm Mailchimp list access (IDs are known; confirm admin access path).
- Confirm portal deep-link URL scheme with portal team.
- Confirm Sitecore export access (Option A) or agree to Option B scrape fallback.

## 32.4 Accessibility and legal

- Confirm external a11y auditor engagement.
- Confirm legal review of privacy, accessibility, complaint, OIA pages.
- Confirm cookie consent requirement (likely not needed with Plausible — verify).
- Confirm accessibility statement ownership and contact.
- Confirm all current site PDFs meet accessibility — if not, agree remediation plan.

## 32.5 Bilingual

- Confirm te reo translation vendor (Te Taura Whiri register).
- Confirm budget and timeline for translation (phase 1 scope: ~30 top-traffic pages).
- Confirm te reo UI microcopy translations (section 22.3).
- Confirm approach for translated te reo announcement strings.

## 32.6 Operations

- Confirm on-call rota post-launch.
- Confirm analytics reviewer role.
- Confirm CMS editor roles and who gets which access.
- Confirm training schedule.
- Confirm launch date and cutover window.
- Confirm communications plan approvals.

## 32.7 Launch

- Confirm launch date.
- Confirm rollback decision-makers and contact paths.
- Confirm sign-off authority for each launch checklist item.
